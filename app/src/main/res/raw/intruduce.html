Verse text can also be partially learned by clicking on word (from-to).



3 Ways to Learn


record
Press the Record button, say text, and at the end it will be compared.


speak +
Speak + press button, text is spoken aloud,
after signal text say, at the end is compared.



words
The first 5 words are spoken aloud and, if said correctly, increased with the number next to them.
The goal is to say everything correctly 3 times.
You can also say more than is said.


check boxes

Signal extra signal tone at the start and end of recording.


pause
If the recording stops too early, you can try this.
Takes practice, but depending on the case, it is a fine extra.

It is easier to recite fluently.
